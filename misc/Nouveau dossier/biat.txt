		- To extend the virtual disk space inside Linux guest:

			4. Shutdown and boot up the VM normally on Centos this time
			5. Login as root user

				> pvs /dev/sda2
					PV         VG     Fmt  Attr PSize  PFree

			7. Now it's time to allocate the entire free space to the Logical Volume (i.e. /dev/mapper/centos-root in this case)
